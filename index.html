Intro

I'm an Electrical Engineering Graduate from the University of Calgary!

I currently work as a Software Engineer for Circle Cardiovascular Imaging, where I work on post image processing software for MRI and CT scans of the heart.

During my Undergrad, I worked for 2 years at SKF Magnetic Bearings, where I had the opportunity to work on both hardware and software in leading edge technology. I have also done exchanges in Xi'an, Hong Kong, and Switzerland. Along with conference trips in Las Vegas and San Francisco.

I program competitively.
